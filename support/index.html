Common Issues

Free trial and subscription

ReceiptRoo includes a 30-day free trial with full access. After the trial ends, a 30-day grace period gives you continued access while you decide whether to subscribe. After the grace period, the app switches to read-only mode — you can still view and delete your existing bills but cannot add new ones or export reports.

To subscribe, tap Subscribe on the paywall screen. To manage or cancel your subscription, go to Settings → Manage Subscription, or visit your Apple ID subscription settings directly.

Subscription not loading

If the paywall shows "Unable to load pricing", check that you have an active internet connection and try tapping Retry. If the issue persists, close and reopen the app. Subscription availability requires a connection to the App Store.

Restore purchases

If you've previously subscribed and your access isn't showing, tap Restore purchases on the paywall screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used when you originally subscribed.

Receipt upload issues

Uploads require a stable internet connection. If a receipt appears stuck processing, wait a few moments and pull down to refresh the Bills screen. Supported formats are JPEG, PNG, and PDF. Files larger than 10 MB may fail to upload.

Extracted data is wrong

ReceiptRoo uses automated OCR to extract data from your receipts. Extracted values (amount, date, merchant name, GST) may occasionally be inaccurate, especially for handwritten or low-quality images. Tap any bill to review and correct the extracted fields manually before relying on them for reporting.

App login issues

If you're unable to sign in, ensure you're using the same sign-in method (email, Sign in with Apple, or Sign in with Google) that you registered with. Check your internet connection and try again. If you've forgotten your password, use the Forgot password link on the login screen.

Delete your account

You can delete your account from within the app: go to Settings → Delete Account. All your data, including bills and receipts, will be permanently removed within 30 days. Note that deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription — cancel it separately through your Apple ID settings before deleting.
