Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ReceiptRoo. ReceiptRoo is a mobile application designed to help individuals and businesses capture, store, and manage receipts.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the app.

By using ReceiptRoo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

This policy is governed by the laws of Australia.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services.

Information You Provide

  • Account Information — email address and any profile details provided during registration
  • Receipt Data — images, PDFs, and other files you upload
  • Extracted Metadata — information derived from receipts such as merchant name, date, amount, and category

Information Collected Automatically

  • Limited Usage Data — non-identifiable information about app interactions (e.g., features used, screens viewed) used to improve performance and reliability
  • Device Information — device type, operating system, and app version for diagnostics
  • Crash Reports — anonymous logs to help identify and fix errors

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, browsing history, or unrelated personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the app's core functionality
  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Process and store receipt data
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Communicate essential service-related updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Build marketing profiles

We do not track you across third-party apps or websites. ReceiptRoo does not engage in cross-app or cross-site tracking.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate the app:

Amazon Web Services (AWS)

  • Authentication (Amazon Cognito)
  • Secure file storage (Amazon S3)
  • Database services (Amazon DynamoDB)
  • Receipt data processing (Amazon Textract)
  • Backend services (AWS Lambda, API Gateway)
  • Email delivery (Amazon SES)

Apple Services

  • Sign in with Apple (authentication option)
  • App Store (payments and subscriptions)

These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for service delivery.

5. Payments and Subscriptions

If ReceiptRoo offers paid features or subscriptions:

  • All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect, store, or process payment card details
  • Billing is managed through your Apple ID account

6. Advertising

ReceiptRoo does not display third-party advertisements.

We do not use ad networks or share your data for advertising purposes.

7. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest in storage systems
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Restricted access to authorised systems only

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure.

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information.

We may share data only in limited situations:

  • With service providers (as described above)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • During a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition), with prior notice

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data — until account deletion (removed within 90 days)
  • Receipt data — until deleted by the user
  • Usage and diagnostic data — up to 12 months

We may retain certain data longer if required by law.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request data export
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time within the app:

Settings → Delete Account

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

You may also contact us at support@receiptroo.com.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including locations where our service providers operate.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

12. Children's Privacy

ReceiptRoo is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is identified, it will be deleted promptly.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users of significant changes via the app or email
