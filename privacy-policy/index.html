Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ReceiptRoo. ReceiptRoo is a mobile application designed to help individuals and businesses capture, store, and manage receipts and invoices.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the app.

By using ReceiptRoo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

This policy is governed by the laws of Australia.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services.

Information You Provide

Account Information

  • Email address and any profile details provided during registration

Receipt and Invoice Data (User Content)

  • Images, PDFs, and documents uploaded by users
  • These documents may contain personal and financial information such as:
    • Names
    • Billing or shipping addresses
    • Merchant details
    • Transaction details (items, amounts, taxes, totals)
  • This information is collected solely as part of the app's core functionality

Extracted Metadata

  • Information derived from receipts such as merchant name, date, amount, and category

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data

  • Non-identifiable information about app interactions (e.g., features used, screens viewed)

Device Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version for diagnostics

Crash Reports

  • Diagnostic data that may be associated with your account or device to help identify and fix issues

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, browsing history, or unrelated personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the app's core functionality
  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Process, store, and display receipts and invoices
  • Extract structured data from uploaded documents
  • Manage your subscription, free trial, and access to app features
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Communicate essential service-related updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Build marketing profiles

ReceiptRoo does not track you across third-party apps or websites.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers:

Amazon Web Services (AWS)

  • Amazon Cognito (authentication)
  • Amazon S3 (secure storage)
  • Amazon DynamoDB (database)
  • Amazon Textract (receipt and invoice data extraction)
  • AWS Lambda & API Gateway (backend services)
  • Amazon SES (email delivery)

Apple Services

  • Sign in with Apple
  • App Store payments and subscriptions

Google

  • Sign in with Google (identity verification via Cognito federation)

RevenueCat

  • Subscription management and entitlement tracking
  • RevenueCat receives subscription event data (purchases, renewals, cancellations) and user identifiers to manage access to app features
  • RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy

These providers process data only to deliver the service and are contractually obligated to protect it.

5. Payments and Subscriptions

  • Payments are processed by Apple via the App Store
  • We do not collect or store payment card details
  • Billing is managed via your Apple ID
  • Subscription status, free trial period, and entitlement state are stored securely to manage your access to app features
  • Subscription lifecycle events (purchases, renewals, cancellations) are received via RevenueCat and used solely to update your access level

6. Advertising

ReceiptRoo does not display advertisements or use advertising networks.

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

8. Sharing of Information
